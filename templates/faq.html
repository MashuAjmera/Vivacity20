Accomodation FAQs
Can I register, but not take accommodation?
The registration amount remains unchanged in both the cases. It is advisable to avail the accommodation facility since there is no other mode of accommodation available in the campus during the fest.
Will all the team members be given accommodation at the same place?
We will try but there is no surety for the same.
What are the accomodation charges?
For every registered individual, the accomodation charge is : ₹150 (One Day), ₹250 (Two Days) and ₹350(Three Days), without meal. The above charge may subject to change. The accomodation charge is to be paid at the registration desk.
Where will the accommodation be provided?
Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well-secured hostels on the campus of LNMIIT.
What is the procedure to be followed after we reach LNMIIT?
You need to come to the Registration Desk, Main Gate along with your college identity card. You will be allotted your place to stay on campus and given a registration booklet.
Registration FAQs
What are the registration charges?
The registration charges and other necessary details regarding the payment are available under the tab "Register" of all specific events at https://vivacity.lnmiit.ac.in.
Can I register, but not take accommodation?
The registration amount remains unchanged in both the cases. It is advisable to avail the accommodation facility since there is no other mode of accommodation available in the campus during the fest.
I want to attend fun/informal events only. Will I have to pay the whole registration fee?
No, you have to pay only for the events. Only if any fun/informal events(excluding concerts) have charges then you have to pay for that specific event, otherwise it's free! In case you want to attend concerts, you need to have a Vivacity pass.
What is the payment procedure for registration of any events?
The payment procedure will be online and offline as well. Those who have been shortlisted, will be sent an email. You will have to report at the registration desk with the payment receipt print out and the mandatory documents. Failing to bring any of the documents may lead to cancellation of your participation. The documents are: College ID card. Printout of the receipt of payment. For Offline payment: Those participants who haven't paid the amount online also have a chance to pay offline for on spot registrations for the events but that completely depend on availability of seats.
How will I get to know about timings and venues for the events during Vivacity?
You will be given a registration booklet that will have all the timings of all the events and their venues. You can also tune to our Vivacity app for live updates.
Concerts & Passes FAQs
What things are not allowed in the concert?
Cosmetics, pointed objects like scissors, knives, etc are not allowed. Make sure you bring your friends along, though! There are arrangements inside for water, along with a food court. So there won’t be any need to carry water! You cannot carry your own food items. However, you can buy food from food courts inside the concert area.
Do I need passes to attend ProShows/Competitions/Informal/Concert Events?
Vivacity will provide you with a booklet and a band as soon as you enter the campus which has to be collected from the registration desk at the main gate. This Booklet and band will act as a pass to all the events other than concerts for non-accommodation holders. For accommodation holders, Vivacity will provide you with a separate booklet and a band and they will act as a pass to all the events including concerts.
Am I eligible to attend a concert?
Anyone having a Vivacity pass is eligible to attend a concert.
How can I attend concerts?
You need to collect passes from the Registration desk.
Can I get passes for my friends by providing their IDs?
No, you will get a pass only on your own ID.
Campus Facilities FAQs
What are the food facilities inside the campus?
There are food stalls available in the arena as long as it's open. Apart from this, there is the campus mess as well as various eateries and canteens in the campus where food is available at subsidized rates.
What about the hospital facility?
There is an institute Medical Unit located near the SAC. In case you fall ill, you are advised to report to us at accommodation desk. We shall make appropriate arrangements for you to be treated at the Medical unit. We shall also be carrying a first aid kit with us, at the accommodation desk.
Are there ATMs inside the campus?
There is one ICICI Bank ATM inside the campus.
How To Reach FAQs
How can we reach to the event?
VIVACITY takes place in The LNM Institute of Information Technology (LNMIIT) campus in Jaipur (state of Rajasthan).
By Air The LNM Institute of Information Technology campus is at a distance of approximately 22km from the airport. LNMIIT has its own transportation facilities to ply to and fro from LNMIIT campus to Jaipur city which starts from Ajmeri Gate, Raja Park, Pink Square mall, Jaipur city. Ajmeri Gate is approximately 3.3km from Jaipur Railway station. Prepaid cabs are available from the airport to the campus.
By Rail The LNM Institute of Information Technology campus is at a distance of a approximately 18km from the Jaipur railway station. LNMIIT has its own transportation facilities to ply to and fro from LNMIIT campus to Jaipur city which starts from Ajmeri Gate, Raja Park, Pink Square mall, Jaipur city. Ajmeri Gate is approximately 3.3km from Jaipur Railway station. Prepaid cabs are available from the Railway station to the campus.
