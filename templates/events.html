The Scribbled Stories Workshop

The posts that you've posted on your stories at 3 am at night thinking about how relatable they are, the posts you've sent to people close to you to remind them how much they mean to you, and the posts that managed to put your everyday things perfectly into a string of few words, everything you've found it on this one single account:@thescribbledstories

We're proud to announce that Scribbled Stories is organising a workshop on the different kinds of micro-tales that everyone thinks about but have a hard time when it comes to putting them into words. Vivacity waits for you to scribble your thoughts out here and who knows, you could be looking at your OWN post on The Scribbled Stories by the end of the day.

Date: February 2nd, 2020
Venue: LT-9, The LNMIIT, Jaipur
Early Bird Registration Fee: INR 250/-

Contact Us

Duologue

Go beyond the boundaries of physical judgment to discover the person behind the blindfold and make friends and connections amongst each other. We intend to provide a platform to explore thoughts, the being(s) and themselves. Conversation plays a potential part in the creation of an interpersonal connection and we aspire to foster this spirit of friendship and storytelling.Come and explore yourself!

Spell Bee

The Literary Committee gives you an opportunity to check your efficiency and excellence in English. Spelling bee gives a chance to show you prowess in English by spelling out words given to you. Answers will be many but the correct one wins it all.

Pictionary

A chance to flaunt your drawing skills or show an unnatural talent of understanding your partner without him/her exchanging any words. The Literary Committee gives you an excellent opportunity to express your thoughts through art and enjoy in a free to participate fun event that lets you put your connect with your partner at test.
