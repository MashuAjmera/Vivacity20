Antakshri

Description :

The registration for the contest shall take place on the spot at the venue.
There shall be multiple rounds. The nature such rounds shall be decided by the organizers.
Each team shall comprise of 3 to 6 members.
The scoring system of each round shall be intimated to the participants during the contest.
The winners shall be decided on the basis of the points earned through the rounds.
The decision of judges and coordinators are final and biding in all cases
