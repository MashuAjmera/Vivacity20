Sahyog

Description

Here we are back with another edition of Sahyog event - SAHYOG 3.0.

The Sahyog event is held annually to cherish the lives of some 50+ deprived especially for l the kids living in slum areas. We,the Sankalp Club of LNMIIT,are planning to make this edition more fruitful for the young faces. This year we are planning to have 3 singing performances followed by a dance and a drama performance by club members.
The programme would be followed by feast for the children. Refreshments, chocolates and gifts will be distributed among the children. SAHYOG would be a 9am -5pm planned programme in any of the Lecture Hall which is a small effort of the SANKALP CLUB to bring smile on the faces of these children.
