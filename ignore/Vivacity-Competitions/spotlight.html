Description

1 GENERAL
• The participants can only use English as well as Hindi in their content. No other language is allowed.
• If the time limit is exceeded, only the part of the content that was served within the time allotted shall be considered.
• The decision of the judges shall be final and binding. No further requests shall be entertained.
• Only solo and duet performances are allowed.
• Use of obscenity/profanity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed.
• The performer shall be held responsible for any kind of misconduct at the location of the event.
• Judging Criteria: Content, Delivery, Gestures, Stage Presence, Audience Engagement and Overall Impact.
• Use of props is allowed, brought in by the participants themselves. The organisers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage.
• Genre: Poetry, Slam, Storytelling, Stand-Up, Music (Vocal and Instrumental), Ghazhals and Shayaris.

2 FIRST ROUND
2.1 COLLEGE PRELIMS
• The participants have to register themselves with the Campus In-charge for the event.
• The participants have to perform on the date and venue provided by their campus in-charge.
• The performance will be video recorded, please make sure the sound is clear.
• Use of any kind of special effects is STRICTLY prohibited and will lead to direct disqualification.
• Time Limit: 2-2.5 minute.

2.2 ONLINE ROUND
• You have to submit a video/audio of you performing one of your pieces, the video/audio maybe even recorded with mobile. Please make sure that the sound is clear.
• Use of any kind of special effects is STRICTLY prohibited and will lead to direct disqualification.
• Time Limit: 2-2.5 minutes.
• The selected performers shall be informed the same via email.
