RULES:
1. Registration is required.
2. It is a team event OR individual participation. Team size limit to 2-3 members,
3. Theme will be provided to the teams in the beginning of the event.
4. Each participant will be provided with a drawing sheet along with other inventories
5. Participants will have to draw something related to theme.
6. The participant will also have to give a written explanation about the art work in around 50 words
7. The time limit for the event is 2 hrs. Exceeding the time limit will result in deduction of marks
8. The judging will be based on beauty, skill, clarity of explanation and relevance to the theme.
9. The decision of the judges and the organizing committee will be final.
