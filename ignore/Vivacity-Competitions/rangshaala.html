Rangshaala

Description

Theatre, the greatest of all art forms, the most immediate way in which a human being can share with another, the sense of what it is to be a human being. Vivacity’20 brings to you “Rangshaala” inviting potpourri of culture it, multitude of expressions and plethora of emotions to celebrate this art of theatre. So, gather your troop to write, perform and present brilliance on stage.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
1. This is the Stage Play Competition.
2. Play could be in English, Hindi or Bi-lingual, or a Musical Drama.
3. The maximum team size allowed is 18 members (including one light in- charge, one sound in-charge and backstage members). Maximum 15 members are allowed on stage. Two teams from same college can participate.
4. The performance can be an original play or an adaptation. There are no constraints regarding that. Inappropriate content and vulgarity/obscenity will NOT be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification.
5. Use of flammable substances like candles, matches or cigarettes or any alcoholic product during performance on stage is not allowed and will result in disqualification.
6. Participants must bring the background music for the play in a standard secondary storage device.
7. In any case judge’s decision will be final.
8. All teams must submit a list of required props on rendition.lnmiit@gmail.com

ROUNDS

❖ Preliminary Round:
1. Every team would have to stage the main part of their play which gives a brief idea about the play.
2. Time Limit: 15 minutes to perform the play (empty stage to empty stage). Not adhering to time limit will result in negative marking.
3. Number of teams that will qualify for the final round will depend on the number of teams participating in the Prelims.

❖ Final Round:
1. Time limit for the play would be 70 minutes (empty stage to empty stage) with a penalty for every extra minute.
2. A slot would be provided for technical run, only for the finalists just before the finals.

JUDGING CRITERIA: Acting, story, overall impact, use of stage.

AWARDS-
1. 1st prize for Best Play
2. Runner-up for Best Play
3. Best Actor
4. Best Actress
