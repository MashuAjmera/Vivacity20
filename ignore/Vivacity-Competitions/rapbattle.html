Rap Battle

Description :

Each contestant shall be allotted 2 to 3 minutes for their performance.
Failing to keep within this limit shall result in deduction of points.
Performance can be in a language of choice of the participant.
The organizers hold the authority to disqualify competitors who are rude, abusive or deemed otherwise unsuitable. The organizers shall not be answerable for such action.
The judges shall have the unanimous and unquestionable say and their decision shall be final.
Judgement shall be based on the lyrics, presence of the performer, delivery and audience appeal.
This event is a subject to number of participations. If ample numbers of participation is not there, it may be cancelled by the coordinators.
