Verismo

Description :   ( INSTRUMENTALS )

• Number of rounds: 1
Vocals are not allowed.
Only one participant is allowed.
You will come with one of your prepared song or something to play and present it in front of the judges.
• Participant has to bring in his/her own instrument.
• You may use any musical instrument.
• Time limit: 5 minutes.
• Judgement Criteria: The participants will be on different instruments,quality of piece chosen to play.
The decision of the Judges and the coordinators shall be final and binding in all cases.
