Ad Mad

Description

1 RULES OF PROCEDURE
• This event is for a group of 3-4 people, and hence you are required to register as a group. In case, you don’t have one already, we will make one for you don’t worry!
• This is a two-tier event wherein the first one is an elimination round which builds up to the finale.
• The first round will consist of a situation which shall be read out to you by the narrator, which shall end on an open but directive note.
• Students will have to suggest a solution (pathway) on how to move forward in that situation and pen it down. Then all teams will read out their written responses, and the judges will eliminate the at least
• The same process mentioned in point 4 will be reiterated thrice and a final of 6-7 teams move to the next and final round.
• This round will now consist of a monologue type narration by a speaker which may either be cryptic or mysterious or something open-ended
• Now each team will be allowed to privately ask the narrator 3 questions which can be very open-ended or irrelevant or just about anything related to the narration and we promise you’ll get an interesting/creative answer from the narrator. Make note that no clarification/repetition/explanation of any part of narration will be given
• Now, once the narrator has visited all the teams and all questions have been answered. You will be given a 10 minute discussion time for mapping out and discussing your plot with your teammates in which no writing will be allowed. Then in the next 45 minutes you’re supposed to write down the piece in the most interesting way you can and submit the paper.

2 JUDGING CRITERIA
• Creativity
• Vocabulary
• Content
• Deviation/Introduction of new elements (-ve)
• Grammatical errors (-ve)
• Problem Tackling skills

3 TERMS AND CONDITIONS
• Registration of the event should be for a group of 2-4
• In case, you cannot form one yourself, register individually, we’ll make one for you
• English shall be the official working language of the whole event.
• No use of electronic devices shall be permitted during the event
• Any judgement delivered shall be final and binding, this is not subject to debate
• Each team will be provided with sheets and pens
• Rounds: Elimination and Finals
• Strict silence is mandatory during any narration and any unruly behaviour regarding this will result in disqualification of the team
• Obscene or discriminatory content is forbidden
