Rangshaala

Description

It's all black and white, still capturing all the colours of emotions! Vivacity'20 presents “Mukh-Abhinaya”, a platform to make your silence, your blare! Hear with your eyes, the words of a silent man. Convey as much as you can, but without noise, Speak as much as you can, but in Silence.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
1. This is the Meme Competition
2. Each team can have maximum of 10 members.
3. Time limit is 15 minutes (empty stage to empty stage) for each team.
4. Negative points will be awarded for exceeding the time limit.
5. The act should not contain any dialogues or lip sync.
6. No act shall contain any offensive, obscene, disrespectful actions or gestures. The act will be immediately stopped, and the entry will be disqualified, if these instructions are not followed.
7. Pre-recorded music is allowed and should be brought in standard secondary storage device.
8. Pre Recorded music shouldn't contain any kind of narrations or voiceovers.
9. All costumes are to be arranged by the participating team. The organizing committee is responsible only for the infrastructural facilities.
10. All teams must submit a list of required props on rendition.lnmiit@gmail.com.

JUDGING CRITERIA:
Acting, Use of Stage, Overall Impact, Story.
Performance could consist of multiple scenes and blackouts.
Note: The decision of the judges will be Final and binding.
