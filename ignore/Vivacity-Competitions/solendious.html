Solendious

Description :   ( WESTERN SOLO )

The contest shall comprise of two rounds:
For the preliminary round:
The participants can perform any song of the genre of their choice.
The time allotted shall be 120 seconds. Failing to keep up with the time limit shall result in deduction of points.
One accompaniment shall be permitted (Participants are requested to bring their own instruments for the performence).
The number of participants qualifying for the final round shall be decided by the organizers on the basis of participation.

For the final round:
The time allotted to each participant is 4-5 minutes.
Each participant is permitted a maximum of 2 accompaniments.
The judges shall have the unanimous and unquestionable say and their decision shall be final. The judges shall base their decision on the following criterions: quality of voice, sense of scale and beat and the quality of rendition.
