Ink The Tee

Description

RULES:
1. Registration is required.
2. It is a team event OR individual participation. Team size limit to 2-3 members.
3. Theme will be provided to the participants in the beginning of the event.
4. Each participant will be provided with a drawing sheet along with other inventories
5. Participants will have to draw something related to theme on the drawing sheet first. The participants will also have to give a written explanation about the art work in around 50 words.
6. The drawing sheets will be evaluated and participants will be selected.
7. Top 20 participants will be given a t-shirt to draw their work upon.
8. The judging will be based on beauty, skill, clarity of explanation and relevance to the theme.
9. The decision of the judges and the organizing committee will be final.
