RULES:
1. Registration is required.
2. It is a team event OR individual participation. Team size limit to 2-3 members. 3. Theme will be provided to the teams at the beginning of the event.
4. Each participant will be provided with a drawing sheet-half of it being black and other half being white, along with other inventories.
5. He/She will have to draw a something related to a certain theme using black and white charcoal/chalk provided to him/her.
6. The participant will also have to give a written explanation about the art work in around 50 words.
7. The judging will be based on beauty, skill, clarity of explanation and relevance to the Theme.
8. The decision of the judges and the organizing committee will be final.
