CS:GO

Description

Whether you’re 2 or 22, video games have fascinated all age groups! It’s time to gear up and compete in a CSGO LAN knockout tournament. The tournament will be played in teams of 5 people and all the matches before the semi-final will be single knockout matches and the semi-finals and finals will be best of 3. The aim of the for the Terrorists is to plant the bomb at either A or B bombsite and successfully detonate the device OR kill all members of the opposing team. The detailed rules are in the rulebook. Looking forward to your enthusiastic participation!

Rules
• Servers will be setup with Warmod, which require teams to ready up. Once both teams are ready, the game begins.
• The game winning conditions is first to have a score of 16. Teams with the highest score at the end wins. In the event of a tie, the game will be reset and overtime will commence.
• Semi- Finals and Finals the match will contain of 3 maps, here the winner will be decided according to the number of maps win or in case to ties.
• All teams must reach the venue within 10 minutes of announcement or the team will forfeit
• Use of third-party cheats or hacks to gain an unfair advantage is strictly not allowed.
• Teams found to be using hacks or cheats will be disqualified from the competition.
MAP FORMAT:
• All maps will be played in the Bomb defuse mode. The aim of the game is the Terrorists to plant the bomb at either A or B bombsite and successfully detonate the device OR kill all members of the enemy team.
• The map pool consists of following maps: de_train, de_inferno, de_mirage, de_dust2, de_nuke, de_overpass, de_cache.
• There will be a knife round to select the in-game teams i.e. Counter terrorist or Terrorist.

SERVER SPECIFIC SETTING:
• Warmup time: 240 sec
• Round time: 115 sec
• Bomb timer: 40 sec
• Start Money: $800
