Duetto

Description :   ( HINDI DUET )

The contest shall comprise of two rounds:
For the preliminary round:
The partners in the duet should belong to the same college.
The participants may perform a song of their choice.
The time allotted to each duet is 2-3 minutes.
Accompaniments or backing tracks are not allowed for this round.
Each participant can exclusively participate in ONE duet.
The number of duets qualifying for the final round shall be decided by the organizers on the basis of participation.

For the final round:
The participants can perform any song of their choice.
The time allotted is 5-6 minutes. Failing to keep within the time limit shall result in deduction of points.
The participants are allowed a maximum of 2 accompaniments.
Backing tracks/karaoke are permitted.
The judges shall have the unanimous and unquestionable say and their decision shall be final. The judges shall base their decision on the following criterions: quality of voice, sense of scale and beat and the quality of rendition.
