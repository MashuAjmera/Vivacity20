Swaranjali

Description :   ( HINDI SOLO )

The contest shall comprise of two rounds:
For the preliminary round:
Contestants shall be provided with a time limit of 120 seconds to sing a song of their choice.
Failing to keep within the time limit will result in the contestant being asked to stop.
One accompaniment shall be permitted (Participants are requested to bring their own instruments for the performence).

For the final round:
The participants can perform any song of their choice.
The time allotted to each contestant is 4 to 5 minutes.
A contestant is allowed up to 2 accompaniments or a karaoke/backing track.
The participants may perform a medley by the artist allotted to them, provided they keep within the stipulated time of 4 to 5 minutes.
The judges shall have the unanimous and unquestionable say and their decision shall be final. The judges shall base their decision on the following criterions: quality of voice, sense of scale and beat and the quality of rendition.
