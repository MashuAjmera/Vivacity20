Mescolanza

Description :   ( GROUP )

There shall be only one round:
The number of vocalists in a group can range from 4 to 10 members and maximum of 4 accompanists are allowed.
The time limit allotted to each group is 7-10 minutes.
Participants are requested to bring their own instruments for the performance.
A group may sing multiple songs. However, they are to keep within the time limit.
The songs performed can be in English or any other Indian language.
The judges shall have the unanimous and unquestionable say and their decision shall be final.
The judges shall base their decision on the following criterions: quality of voice, sense of scale and beat and the quality of rendition.
